Parking Information

Visitors to campus may park in the following University parking lots, except where prohibited by signs or markings:

Lots A, B, C-North, D, and I

Please refer to the Visitor Parking Map

Note: Visitors may NOT park in any of the lots designated for "Housing Residents Only."

If you are parked in one of the visitor approved lots (Lots A, B, C-North, D, and I) and you receive a ticket for failure to display a parking permit, you may have the violation waived:
  • On the reverse side of the ticket, please check 'visitor' and fill in your name and address;
  • Return the ticket to the following address, including a short statement that you were on campus attending the Cyber Defense and Disaster Recovery Conference.
Cashier's Office
University of Illinois at Springfield
One University Plaza, PAC, 184
Springfield, Illinois 62703

Please note that you may request that a ticket be waived only if you were cited for not displaying a parking permit. If you receive a ticket for a different violation, the ticket cannot be waived.
