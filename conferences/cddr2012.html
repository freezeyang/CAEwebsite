About the Theme: Come join us as we look at our increasing dependence on well-behaved computers both today and looking to the future. We will consider when and how we should question the risks from IT being involved in everything from our basic privacy to life-critical systems. As in past years, we also endeavor to offer other topics at the conference to diversify and enrich your experience.
