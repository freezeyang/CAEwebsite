ATTENTION:
TO THOSE REGISTERED FOR SANS TRAINING

The SANS training requires you to bring your own laptop computer for training use in the Hands On Sessions.  The laptop computer you bring must meet the following specs:

 *   Fully charged and able to hold that charge for the duration of the sessions (two hours) **
 *   Windows-based OS
 *   Administrator authority on the laptop you bring

If you are unable to bring a laptop computer to use for training, you are still welcome to occupy your reserved seat and take notes. However, know that you will not be able to participate in the same way as those with a laptop who are able to interact fully with the materials and instructor.

** Limited wall outlets are available to connect to for charging. But due to the number of participants in the SANS sessions, there is no guarantee of outlet availability.
