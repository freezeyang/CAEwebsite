Cyber Defense and Disaster Recovery Conference 2007

03/09/2007 - 03/09/2007
University of Illinois Springfield
Public Affairs Center, Conference Room C/D
Springfield, IL  62703
Cost: $30.00

Overview:

The Cyber Defense and Disaster Recovery Conference is a one-day program
addressing the critical issue of disaster recovery. This year’s theme
is a pandemic crisis. Attendees participate in a simulated pandemic tabletop
exercise.
