Cyber Defense and Disaster Recovery Conference 2006

03/10/2006 - 03/10/2006
University of Illinois Springfield
Public Affairs Center, Studio Theater, First Floor
Springfield, IL  62703
Cost: $30.00

Overview:

The Cyber Defense and Disaster Recovery Conference was a one-day program addressing the critical issue of emergency response and disaster recovery. Keynote speakers gave presentations of current issues. Attendees learned effective techniques and participate in a tabletop exercise based on a realistic disaster scenario.

Conference brochure

Dr. Newton Howard: Emerging Digital Threats in Service-
Oriented Architectures
Center for Advanced Defense Studies
Washington, D.C.

Dr. Roy Campbell: Protecting the Power Grid
Sohaib and Sara Abbasi Professor in Computer Science, UICU

Dr. Sviatoslav Braynov: The Perils of Coordinated Attacks
