Ms. Mary Sheila Tracy

Ms. Mary Sheila Tracy regularly teaches introductory programming courses in different languages, as well as upper-division courses in foundational topics for computer science and usability engineering both on ground and online.  She serves as the undergraduate coordinator.   Her current research interests are online computer science education and usability engineering.  Prior to being invited to join the faculty, Mary Sheila worked as a programmer/analyst for Sangamon State University/UIS/AITS for twelve years.  She earned her Master of Arts in Computer Science from UIS.  She made her first conference presentation last spring.
