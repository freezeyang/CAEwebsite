Dr. Ping Deng

Dr. Ping Deng has published more than 15 papers in journals, books, and conference proceedings. She was also an invited speaker for several DIMACS workshops. She served as a reviewer for a number of journals, books, conferences, and workshops. Her research interests include privacy, database systems, data mining, and bioinformatics.
