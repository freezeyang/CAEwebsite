Center for Systems Security and Information Assurance (CSSIA)

Ted Mims, Co-Principal Investigator, National Science Foundation (NSF), Award number 0702872, Continuing grant , Total amount $1,402,184, 9/30/2007 –10/01/2009.

Abstract: The Center for Systems Security and Information Assurance (CSSIA) was established in 2003 as an ATE regional center for cybersecurity education (NSF Award No. 0302612). With renewed NSF funding, this center is continuing to develop, adapt, and enhance courses and curricula and offer training to community college and university faculty and students across a six-state region in the Midwest.
